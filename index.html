    团队选取的调研地是山东威海的乳山市社区课堂。大学生以及青年志愿者，开展“七彩假期”公益课堂，为青少年儿童提供形式多样、丰富多彩的陪伴活动。我们团队可以为学生提供陪伴和学习指导，充分发挥我们作为大学生的优势以及帮助家长解决孩子缺少陪伴的问题。

    Our research site is focused on the community classrooms in Rushan, Weihai, Shandong. By initiating the "Colorful Holidays" public welfare program, university students and young volunteers offer diverse and enriching companionship activities for children and youth. Our team aims to provide company and academic guidance to students, leveraging our strengths as university students and assisting parents in addressing the issue of inadequate companionship for their children during holidays.
